Comfort Fabric Conditioner

Comfort fabric conditioner is a popular laundry product known for its ability to make clothes soft, fresh, an d pleasant to wear. One of the key benefits of Comfort fabric conditioner is its ability to soften clothes, making them feel gentle and comfortable against the skin. It also imparts a fresh fragrance that lasts long after the clothes have been washed.

Brand's Target Audience

  • Geography: Targeted towards consumers in urban and semi-urban areas in India.
  • Demography: Middle to upper-class households.
  • Psychography: Conscious consumers, scent enthusiasts, value for money consumers.

Derived Problem Statement (Based on research)

Affordability poses a barrier for lower-middle-class and below individuals in accessing Comfort Fabric Conditioner, highlighting economic challenges for household essentials.

Ideation for Solution

  • Target Audience: Middle and lower middle-class individuals.
  • Benefits of Comfort fabric conditioner:
    1. Extended lifespan of clothes.
    2. Reduction in the frequency of purchasing new clothes.
    3. Effective care for clothes, leaving them soft, fragrant, and in pristine condition.
  • Approach: Convince the target audience that using a fabric conditioner is a wise investment.
